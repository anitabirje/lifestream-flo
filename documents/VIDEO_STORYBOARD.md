## Visual Style Guide

### Color Palette
- **Sarah (Mom)**: #3B82F6 (Blue)
- **Tom (Dad)**: #10B981 (Green)
- **Emma (Daughter)**: #8B5CF6 (Purple)
- **Jake (Son)**: #F59E0B (Orange)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (White)
- **Text**: #1F2937 (Dark Gray)
- **Accents**: #F3F4F6 (Light Gray)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Inter Bold, 24-32px
- **Body**: Inter Regular, 14-16px
- **Labels**: Inter Medium, 12-14px

### Animations
- **Transitions**: 300ms ease-in-out
- **Hover Effects**: Scale 1.02, shadow
- **Modal Entrance**: Fade + scale from 0.95
- **Toast Notifications**: Slide from top

### Camera Movements
- **Pan**: Smooth, 2-3 seconds
- **Zoom**: Gradual, 1-2 seconds
- **Cuts**: Clean, no jarring transitions
- **Scroll**: Natural speed, not too fast
