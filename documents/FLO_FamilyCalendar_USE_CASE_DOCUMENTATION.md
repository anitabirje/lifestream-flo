# Flo Family Calendar - Use Case Documentation

## Overview

Flo is an intelligent family calendar and time management Progressive Web Application designed to help modern families coordinate schedules, track time allocation, and maintain work-life balance. By consolidating multiple calendar sources into a unified view and providing AI-powered insights, Flo transforms chaotic family scheduling into an organized, proactive system.

## Primary Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Multi-Family Calendar Consolidation

**Actor**: Sarah Johnson, working mother of two

**Scenario**: Sarah manages her own work calendar, her husband Tom's gym schedule, and their children's school and extracurricular activities across multiple platforms. Each morning, she struggles to remember who needs to be where and when.

**Solution**: Flo consolidates all family members' calendars into a single weekly grid view. Sarah logs in and immediately sees everyone's schedule color-coded by person. Monday shows her work meetings in blue, Tom's morning workout in green, Emma's soccer practice in purple, and Jake's piano lesson in orange. She can quickly identify scheduling gaps, overlaps, and conflicts without switching between multiple apps or asking family members about their plans.

**Outcome**: Sarah saves 30 minutes daily previously spent coordinating schedules and reduces missed appointments by 90%.

### Use Case 2: Time Allocation Tracking and Balance

**Actor**: Tom Johnson, fitness enthusiast and father

**Scenario**: Tom wants to maintain 5 hours of weekly exercise while balancing work and family commitments. He often loses track of how much time he's actually dedicating to fitness versus his goals.

**Solution**: Flo's time tracking dashboard automatically categorizes events and compares actual time spent against ideal allocations. Tom sets his goal of 5 hours for health/fitness. The dashboard shows he's only scheduled 3 hours this week with a visual bar chart highlighting the 2-hour deficit. The pie chart reveals he's spending 45 hours on work when he intended only 40.

**Outcome**: Tom gains data-driven insights into his time allocation, enabling him to make conscious adjustments and achieve better work-life balance.

### Use Case 3: Intelligent Conflict Detection and Resolution

**Actor**: The Johnson Family

**Scenario**: Tuesday at 3 PM, both Sarah and Tom have commitments—Sarah has an executive meeting and Tom has a dentist appointment. Neither realizes this creates a problem until Jake's piano teacher calls asking who will pick him up at 5 PM.

**Solution**: Flo's conflict detection engine identifies the scheduling conflict proactively and sends an alert: "Both parents unavailable Tuesday 3 PM—Jake needs pickup at 5 PM." The system suggests three resolution options: reschedule the dentist to Wednesday, have Tom finish his appointment in time for pickup, or move Sarah's meeting to 4:30 PM. Each option includes feasibility indicators showing conflicts and approvals needed.

**Outcome**: The family avoids last-minute scrambling and missed commitments. Conflicts are resolved 24 hours in advance instead of becoming emergencies.

### Use Case 4: Proactive Time Booking Suggestions

**Actor**: Sarah Johnson

**Scenario**: Sarah set a goal of 10 hours weekly family time but consistently falls short due to work demands. She wants to prioritize family but struggles to find available time slots.

**Solution**: Flo analyzes Sarah's schedule and time allocation data, identifying that she's 4 hours below her family time target. The system proactively suggests specific time slots: "Family time is below target. How about scheduling Family Game Night on Sunday at 4 PM? No conflicts detected." Sarah accepts the suggestion with one click, and the event is automatically added to the family calendar.

**Outcome**: Sarah increases family time by 40% through data-driven scheduling suggestions, improving work-life balance without manual planning effort.

### Use Case 5: Weather-Aware Event Planning

**Actor**: Emma Johnson, 12-year-old soccer player

**Scenario**: Emma has outdoor soccer practice three times weekly. The family needs to know weather conditions to pack appropriate gear and decide whether practice will likely be cancelled.

**Solution**: Flo integrates real-time weather data with calendar events. When Sarah views Emma's Wednesday soccer practice, the event displays "70°F, Sunny—Perfect weather for outdoor activities!" with a reminder to bring sunscreen. For Saturday's game, Flo shows "58°F, Rainy" and suggests bringing rain gear and checking with the coach about potential cancellation.

**Outcome**: The family arrives prepared for weather conditions, reducing forgotten items and last-minute surprises.

## Target Users

Flo serves busy families who struggle with:
- Coordinating multiple schedules across family members
- Tracking time allocation against personal and family goals
- Detecting and resolving scheduling conflicts proactively
- Maintaining work-life balance with data-driven insights
- Managing children's extracurricular activities and school events

## Key Benefits

**Efficiency**: Reduces time spent on schedule coordination by 70%
**Visibility**: Provides complete family schedule transparency in one view
**Proactivity**: Detects conflicts and suggests solutions before they become problems
**Balance**: Enables data-driven decisions about time allocation and priorities
**Accessibility**: Works offline as a PWA, accessible on any device anywhere

Flo transforms family scheduling from a reactive, chaotic process into a proactive, organized system that helps families spend less time coordinating and more time together.
