Features

Everything a busy family needs

📰

Newsletter AI Agent

Upload any school newsletter PDF and Flo extracts every date and deadline automatically using Amazon Textract + Bedrock.

⚡

Clash Detection

Flo spots scheduling conflicts before they happen and suggests resolution paths — like asking your partner to cover pickup.

⚖️

Invisible Load Dashboard

See how household coordination is distributed between partners. Make the invisible load visible — and shareable.

🎯

Values-Based Scheduling

Set weekly goals for family, health, wellbeing and upskilling. Flo checks you're protecting time for what matters.

🌤️

Weather Intelligence

Flo checks UV, AQI and rain forecasts for every outdoor event. Never forget sunscreen for the 7am soccer game again.

COMING SOON
🎙️

Voice Briefings

Your Sunday summary delivered as a warm 60-second audio brief via Amazon Polly — like a PA for family life.
